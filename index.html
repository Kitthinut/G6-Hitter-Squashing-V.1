Welcome to Hitter_Squashing Simulation Webapp Project

Welcome to the Hitter_Squashing Squash Ball Calculation Simulation — your easy-to-use tool for simulating squash ball in FRA162-163 Project. Whether you are a student learning physics, a sports enthusiast, or non-sleep panda-eye some FRAB person,

this web application is designed to provide accurate calculations of distance traveled, total time, and the landing zone based on key input parameters. Using simple inputs like the launch angle, velocity, racket mass, and coefficient of restitution,

the app will calculate the ball’s flight time and range, helping you visualize where the ball will land in different zones. This interactive tool also allows you to save and reset your settings, ensuring a customizable and user-friendly experience.

Start by entering your parameters in the Calculator section, and watch as the app computes the results, displaying them in a clear and informative way. Whether you're debugging your squash ball hitter machine or analyzing your calculation theory , this calculator will assist you in making quick, accurate predictions.
